When I moved from Minneapolis to Delhi in 2015, I anticipated many of India’s challenges - but didn't realize that breathing would slowly be killing me too.

Air Pollution 101

It's not just an India (or China) problem either. The WHO says that more than 6.5 million people die every year worldwide due to air pollution! Air pollution sucks but keeping an eye on the air quality in your city and figuring out how to protect yourself should be straight forward. That's why I made this map for you. Although it wouldn't be possible without OpenAQ, the World Health Organization, NASA and EmojiOne.
